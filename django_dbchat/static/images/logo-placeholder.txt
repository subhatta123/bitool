ConvaBI Logo Placeholder
=========================

To add your ConvaBI logo:

1. Save your logo image as: static/images/convabi-logo.png
2. Recommended dimensions: 64x64px or higher (square format works best)
3. Format: PNG with transparent background preferred

The logo will automatically appear in:
- Navigation bar (32px height)
- Home page hero section (64px height)
- Loading and error pages

If the logo file is not found, the text "ConvaBI" will be displayed instead.
